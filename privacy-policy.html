Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. Learn how BlockIT Pro protects your data.

Last updated: June 21, 2025
Privacy First: BlockIT Pro is designed with privacy at its core. We do not collect, store, or share your personal browsing data. Our ad blocking happens locally on your device.

This Privacy Policy describes Our policies and procedures on the collection, use and disclosure of Your information when You use the Service and tells You about Your privacy rights and how the law protects You.

We use Your Personal data to provide and improve the Service. By using the Service, You agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Data Collection and Privacy Principles

Core Privacy Principle: BlockIT Pro operates on a "privacy-first" approach. We do not track your browsing habits, collect personal information, or store your browsing data.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • Your browsing history or visited websites
  • Personal information (name, email, address, phone numbers)
  • Search queries or form data
  • Login credentials or passwords
  • Financial or payment information
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Social media profiles or personal identifiers

Minimal Data We May Collect

Extension Usage Statistics (Anonymous)

We may collect minimal, anonymized usage statistics to improve our service:

  • Number of ads blocked (aggregate counts only)
  • Extension version information
  • Browser type and version (for compatibility)
  • General error logs (no personal data included)

Website Analytics

Our website (blockit.dharaik.in) may collect standard web analytics:

  • Page views and visit duration
  • Referrer information (which site directed you to us)
  • Device type and screen resolution
  • General geographic region (country/state level only)

How BlockIT Pro Works

Local Processing

All ad blocking functionality operates locally on your device:

  • Ad filtering rules are processed locally in your browser
  • No browsing data is sent to external servers
  • Blocked content lists are updated from trusted, public sources
  • Your browsing remains completely private

Filter Lists and Updates

BlockIT Pro uses publicly available filter lists to block ads:

  • Filter lists are downloaded from reputable sources (EasyList, uBlock Origin, etc.)
  • Updates happen automatically without sending any personal data
  • No custom or personal blocking rules are shared externally

Use of Your Personal Data

The Company may use the minimal data we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our Service, including to monitor the performance and effectiveness of ad blocking
  • To improve our Service: Understanding which filter rules are most effective to enhance blocking capabilities
  • To provide customer support: Helping users troubleshoot issues with the extension
  • To detect and prevent abuse: Ensuring the extension is used appropriately and preventing malicious activity
  • For legal compliance: Meeting legal obligations while maintaining user privacy

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We Do Not Share Personal Data

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal data to third parties. The minimal anonymous data we collect is never shared with:

  • Advertising companies
  • Data brokers
  • Marketing companies
  • Social media platforms
  • Analytics companies (beyond basic website analytics)

Limited Exceptions

We may disclose information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Legal requirements: If required by law, court order, or government request
  • Security threats: To protect against fraud, abuse, or security threats
  • Business transfer: In the event of a merger or acquisition (users will be notified)

Data Security and Storage

Security Measures

  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Minimal data storage with industry-standard security practices
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • No sensitive personal data is stored on our servers

Data Retention

  • Anonymous usage statistics: Retained for up to 2 years for service improvement
  • Website analytics: Retained according to Google Analytics policies (typically 26 months)
  • Support communications: Retained for up to 3 years for quality assurance

Your Privacy Rights

User Controls

  • Disable analytics: You can opt out of anonymous usage statistics in extension settings
  • Uninstall: Remove the extension at any time from your browser
  • Data deletion: Contact us to request deletion of any data we may have
  • Transparency: Request information about what data we have collected

European Users (GDPR Rights)

If you are in the European Union, you have additional rights:

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to rectification (correction) of data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13. If You are a parent or guardian and You are aware that Your child has provided Us with Personal Data, please contact Us.

Third-Party Services

Chrome Web Store

BlockIT Pro is distributed through the Chrome Web Store, which has its own privacy policy. Google may collect information about extension installations and usage according to their policies.

Filter List Providers

We use filter lists from trusted sources like EasyList and uBlock Origin. These are downloaded directly from their servers, and no personal data is shared in this process.

Website Analytics

Our website uses Google Analytics to understand visitor behavior. You can opt out using browser settings or Google's opt-out tools.

International Data Transfers

As we collect minimal data, international transfers are limited. Any data processed outside your country will be protected according to this privacy policy and applicable laws.

  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • Providing notice through the extension (for significant changes)

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
